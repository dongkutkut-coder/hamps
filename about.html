ABOUT EVO

Premium helmets designed for protection, comfort, and style. Chih-Tong EVO helmet was established in Taiwan in 1997. We are a professional safety helmets manufacturer specialising in motorcycle, bicycle and sports helmets. The Evo series of helmets were designed with one goal in mind: to be the best flight helmets on the market.

EVO strives to continuously create quality helmets focusing on safety and protection to motorcycle riders as this is the primary purpose of a helmet. Moreover, the team behind EVO helmets creates with passion, commitment to excellence and continuous improvement. Thus, the tagline "Evolution Never Stops" came upon. EVO envisions to bring the brand into the world stage and give pride to the Filipino people.
